## Stat Pumping Priority

Magician is a **SPI-primary caster class**. Gear is dominated by SPI and TAL,
with moderate STR and AGI needed for shared boots/gauntlets/bracelets mid-game onward.

| Priority | Stat | Rationale |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **SPI** | Required by every equip slot — weapons, armor, orbs, rings, amulets. Largest gap between milestones. |
| 2 | **TAL** | Required by weapons and armor throughout. Caps at 90 from level 65–108, then jumps to 95–100 at 110+. |
| 3 | **STR** | Appears from level 30 on weapons/phantoms, then on boots/gauntlets/bracelets from level 44+. Caps around 100–115. |
| 4 | **AGI** | Only needed for bracelets (from level 9) and boots/gauntlets (from level 50+). Scales gradually to ~105. |

## Stat Allocation Guide (per level-up)

- **Levels 1–29:** Pure SPI → TAL. No STR or AGI needed. Max SPI for damage output.
- **Levels 30–54:** SPI still dominant. STR starts creeping in (35→70). Keep AGI at ~20 for bracelets.
- **Levels 55–89:** SPI and TAL both demand attention. STR rises to ~100. AGI climbs to ~90.
- **Levels 90–113:** SPI continues to 255. TAL stays at 90 until 110+ where it jumps to 95–100. STR plateaus at ~100–115. AGI reaches ~105.
