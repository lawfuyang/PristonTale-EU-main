## Stat Pumping Priority

Pikeman is a **STR-primary melee class**. All gear requires STR and TAL.
If going Javelin route, AGI becomes equally important from level 55+.

| Priority | Stat | Rationale |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **STR** | Required by every equip slot. Largest gap between milestones. |
| 2 | **TAL** | Required by weapons, armor, shield, gauntlets. Caps at 90 until 110. |
| 3 | **AGI** | Required by Javelins, Boots, Gauntlets, Bracelets. Scales aggressively from 55+. |
| 4 | **SPI** | Only needed for accessories (rings/amulets) and some Boots/Gauntlets from 55+. Minimal for pure melee. |

## Stat Allocation Guide (per level-up)

- **Levels 1–54:** Pump STR → TAL. Keep AGI at minimum for boots/bracelets.
- **Levels 55–90:** STR still priority, but AGI spikes hard if using Javelins. TAL usually fine at 90.
- **Levels 90+:** STR continues climbing to 248. TAL jumps to 95–97 at 110+. AGI can reach 200.
