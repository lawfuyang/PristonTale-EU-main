## Stat Pumping Priority

Pikeman is a **STR-primary melee class**. All gear requires STR and TAL.
AGI is needed for Boots, Gauntlets, and Bracelets from early levels.
SPI is required for Rings, Amulets, and Sheltoms — the only SPI-gated slots.

| Priority | Stat | Rationale |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **STR** | Required by every equip slot. Largest gap between milestones. |
| 2 | **TAL** | Required by weapons, armor, gauntlets. Caps at 90 until 110. |
| 3 | **AGI** | Required by Boots, Gauntlets, Bracelets. Scales from 30→105. |
| 4 | **SPI** | Required for Rings, Amulets, Sheltoms. No combat gear needs SPI. Scales from 28→110. |

## Stat Allocation Guide (per level-up)

- **Levels 1–29:** Pump STR → TAL. Keep AGI at minimum for boots/bracelets. Keep SPI at ~28–32 for early rings/amulets.
- **Levels 30–54:** STR still priority. SPI climbs to ~52 (ring+3). AGI stays moderate (~50–58).
- **Levels 55–90:** STR climbs to 208. TAL caps at 90. AGI plateaus at 90 from level 75. SPI rises to 85.
- **Levels 90+:** STR continues to 248. TAL jumps to 95–97 at 110+. AGI edges to 105 from bracelets. SPI reaches 110 at 108.
